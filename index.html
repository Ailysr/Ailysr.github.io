I am a Master's student at the Northwestern Polytechnical University School of Aeronautics, focusing on aircraft safety design and eVTOL (electric Vertical Take-Off and Landing) technology research.

My research interests are primarily concentrated in aircraft safety design, emerging aviation technologies, and eVTOL system development. As a graduate student, I am committed to improving aircraft safety performance through innovative design methods and advanced analytical techniques.

During my time at Northwestern Polytechnical University, I actively participate in various research projects and continuously learn and explore in related fields. My goal is to contribute to the development of future aviation technology, particularly in urban air mobility and novel aircraft design.

I have a solid foundation in engineering technology and am proficient in relevant design software and analytical tools. I also actively follow industry trends and share learning insights and research progress through various platforms.
