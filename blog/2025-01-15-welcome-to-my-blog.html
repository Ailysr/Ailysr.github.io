My Background

I completed my undergraduate studies at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, where I developed a strong foundation in aerospace engineering principles. Currently, I'm pursuing my Master's degree at Northwestern Polytechnical University, focusing specifically on the safety aspects of aircraft design and the emerging field of eVTOL technology.

My research interests include:

  • Aircraft structural safety and reliability
  • eVTOL system design and certification
  • Flight control systems and autonomous operations
  • Risk assessment and safety management in aviation
